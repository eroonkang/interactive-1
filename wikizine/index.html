A general suggestion for your layout is to figure things out from the big boxes to small boxes.

  1. Identify 1 or more vertically repeating content chunks in your design. Consider them sections.
  2. Inside each section, if content boxes need to be side-by-side (columns), use grid or flexbox.
  3. Inside those columnns, generally place items top to bottom (block elements).
  4. Occasionally override with position: absolute and the like.
